## What Do They Actually Sell?

Deasil (operating their client portal under the "Deasil Works" brand) offers three main categories of services through their store:

**Dedicated Servers** — Physical servers on Dell PowerEdge hardware, dedicated exclusively to you. They have a hardware guarantee: if it breaks, they replace it. Specs range from entry-level Atom and Xeon configurations up to higher-tier dual-processor setups.

**Colocation** — You bring your own server; they rack it in one of their secure LA facilities. You get 24-hour on-site security, locking cabinets, managed and unmanaged options, and your choice of Ethernet or fiber to the cabinet.

**VPS Hosting** — Xen-based virtual private servers for teams that want isolated resources without the full commitment of a bare-metal dedicated server.

There's also a **cPanel VPS License** add-on for those who prefer the familiar cPanel environment.

## Network: The Stuff That Actually Matters

Their network is genuinely enterprise-grade. The BGP setup is directly connected or one hop from all major carriers. They use Foundry and Cisco routers with fiber internal links. Their private LAN over SONET can connect to data centers in North America, Europe, and Japan — useful if your infrastructure spans multiple regions.

The 99.999% network uptime SLA is the kind of number you typically see quoted by carriers and tier-1 providers, not smaller hosting companies. Whether you're in their LA flagship locations or at DAL1 in Dallas, you're on the same backbone.
